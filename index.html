Wei Xiao

I am currently a Postdoc Associate at the MIT Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory under the supervision of Prof. Daniela Rus. I got my Ph.D. degree in Systems Engineering at Boston University under the supervision of Prof. Christos G. Cassandras and Prof. Calin Belta.

My research interest includes safety-critical control theory and trustworthy machine learning with applications to robotics and multi-agent systems.
